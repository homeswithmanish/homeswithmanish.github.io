🌁 CITY GUIDE

Living in Fremont, California

Silicon Valley access with legendary Mission schools

Median SFH: $1.4M–$2M+

Range reflects recent Zillow ZHVI data · live figure loads when available · see the full market table

Fremont is the East Bay's direct line to Silicon Valley: Tesla's factory, Warm Springs BART, and bridges to the Peninsula make it the shortest big-tech commute on this side of the Bay. Median values vary more by neighborhood than any other city I serve — Mission San Jose commands a large premium over the citywide range.

For many buyers the decision is school-driven: Mission San Jose High's reputation pulls families from across the Bay Area, and homes in its boundary are priced accordingly.

Mission schools

Mission San Jose High is among the highest-performing public high schools in California.

Tech proximity

Tesla in town; Google, Apple, and Meta reachable via I-880, Dumbarton, or BART.

BART

Fremont and Warm Springs stations connect to the entire Bay Area network.

Diversity

One of the most culturally diverse large cities in America, with food and festivals to match.

EDUCATION

Schools in Fremont

Fremont Unified School District (FUSD). FUSD is large and varies by attendance area. The Mission San Jose feeder pattern is nationally known; American, Irvington, and Washington serve other strong neighborhoods.

  • Mission San Jose High School
  • American High School
  • Irvington High School
  • Hopkins Junior High
GETTING AROUND

Commute & Connectivity

Fremont has the South Bay's best East Bay commute: two BART stations, I-880, and the Dumbarton Bridge to the Peninsula.

  • Warm Springs / Fremont BART: network-wide access
  • Tesla factory: in-town
  • Google/Meta (Peninsula): ~30–50 min via Dumbarton
  • San Jose: ~20–35 min via I-880
NEIGHBORHOODS

Where to Look in Fremont

Mission San Jose

The school-premium district — median values here often exceed $2M.

Ardenwood

Northwest Fremont, popular with Peninsula commuters using the Dumbarton Bridge.

Warm Springs

Newer development around the BART station and innovation district.

Niles & Irvington

Historic districts with character homes and walkable pockets.

INVESTOR LENS

Fremont for Investors

Fremont rents strongly to tech tenants and appreciates on Silicon Valley's coattails. Yields are moderate; school-zone properties hold value best in downturns.

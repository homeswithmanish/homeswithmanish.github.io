🍷 CITY GUIDE

Living in Livermore, California

Wine country value on the Tri-Valley's east edge

Median SFH: $1.2M–$1.5M

Range reflects recent Zillow ZHVI data · live figure loads when available · see the full market table

Livermore delivers the Tri-Valley lifestyle at a meaningful discount: median single-family values typically run $1.2M–$1.5M, and buyers get a revitalized downtown, 50+ wineries, and a genuine sense of community.

It's also a two-sided market — families upgrading within the Tri-Valley on one side, and investors chasing the area's better rental yields on the other. South Livermore near the vineyards commands the premium.

Value

Meaningfully more attainable than Pleasanton or San Ramon while staying inside the Tri-Valley.

Wine country

50+ wineries in the Livermore Valley AVA, plus a lively renovated downtown.

Employers

Lawrence Livermore and Sandia national labs anchor thousands of stable, high-paying jobs.

Yields

Among the better gross rental yields in the Tri-Valley — a frequent pick for my investor clients.

EDUCATION

Schools in Livermore

Livermore Valley Joint Unified School District (LVJUSD). LVJUSD performs solidly, with Granada and Livermore High anchoring two feeder patterns. School quality is strong relative to the price discount versus neighboring districts.

  • Granada High School
  • Livermore High School
  • East Avenue Middle School
  • Mendenhall Middle School
GETTING AROUND

Commute & Connectivity

Livermore is the eastern end of the Tri-Valley commute: ACE train service, I-580 access, and a short drive to BART.

  • ACE train from Vasco Rd / downtown to Silicon Valley
  • Dublin/Pleasanton BART: ~20 min drive
  • Lawrence Livermore & Sandia labs: in-town employers
  • San Francisco: ~60–80 min door to door in commute hours
NEIGHBORHOODS

Where to Look in Livermore

Springtown

North-side value neighborhoods popular with first-time buyers and investors.

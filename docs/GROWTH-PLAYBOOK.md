# Growth Playbook — Phase 2
Companion to the city pages + calculators shipped 2026-07-03. Owner: Manish.

## 1. Google Business Profile (GBP) — one-time setup (~45 min)
GBP is the highest-ROI local SEO asset an agent has. Checklist:

- [ ] Claim/verify profile as **"Manish Anand, Realtor — Homes With Manish"** (Service Area Business; hide street address, list the 8 cities as service areas).
- [ ] Primary category: *Real estate agent*. Secondary: *Real estate consultant*.
- [ ] Website: homeswithmanish.com · Phone: (408) 707-5324 — must match the site exactly (NAP consistency).
- [ ] Description: lead with "data-driven East Bay real estate" + the 8 city names (750 chars max).
- [ ] Photos: headshot, logo, and 8+ real local photos (city landmarks you took, open houses). No stock.
- [ ] Services: add each service card from the site (First-Time Buyer Guidance, Investment Analysis, Seller Representation…).
- [ ] Q&A: seed 5 questions yourself using the site FAQ (you can post and answer your own).
- [ ] Weekly: one GBP post (reuse newsletter market snippet — 5 min/week; posts expire, cadence matters).
- [ ] Link the profile in the site footer once live.

## 2. Review-generation flow (starts end of July when real reviews land)
1. Day of closing: text the client a direct GBP review link (`g.page/r/...` short link from the GBP dashboard) with a personal note.
2. Day 3: email fallback with the same link (this becomes a CRM automation in Phase 3).
3. Every review gets a personalized owner reply within 48h (SEO signal + prospect trust).
4. Once ≥3 real Google reviews exist: re-add the testimonials section to the site quoting them ("via Google") and restore AggregateRating schema **fed by the real GBP numbers** (closes D-006's loop).
